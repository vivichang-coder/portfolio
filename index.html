About

Hi, I'm Vivi Chang — a UX Designer and Researcher with a background in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

I specialize in designing complex systems, turning high-density features and fragmented workflows into clear, scalable user experiences.

At QNAP, I worked across B2B and B2C products (file management, multimedia, and surveillance platforms), where I led UX research and design initiatives to improve usability, reduce cognitive load, and drive feature adoption. I partner closely with PMs and engineers to identify risks early, translate insights into product strategy, and ensure solutions are both usable and feasible.

My approach combines research, system thinking, and design execution — not just to improve interfaces, but to make complex products understandable, efficient, and scalable.
